My Favorite Internet Week 1

  • mfi1

    YeahBoi

    This website is a little jump course game where you add i's to your phrase by surviving. It's done with a simple style, looking mostly like text. Hitting other letters gives you powerups with different effects.

  • mfi2

    Text Art

    This website lets you create art with type. You can choose what type gets put out, what color it is, what the background looks like, and so on. However, I have noticed one flaw, and that is when I resize my window, it wipes all my progress. I'm not sure if this is exclusive to me or if it is from certain browsers.
